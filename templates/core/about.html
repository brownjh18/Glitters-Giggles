About Glitters & Giggles

We're passionate about creating magical experiences for children and families across Uganda. With years of experience in children's event management, we bring joy and learning to every occasion.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Glitters & Giggles began with a simple mission: to make children's events more than just celebrations, but opportunities for growth, learning, and unforgettable memories.

What started as a small team organizing birthday parties has grown into a comprehensive children's event management company serving Kampala, Wakiso, and Entebbe. We believe that every child deserves an extraordinary experience that sparks imagination and creates lasting joy.

Our Values

What drives everything we do

Every event is designed with the child's joy and development in mind. We create experiences that nurture creativity and build confidence.
